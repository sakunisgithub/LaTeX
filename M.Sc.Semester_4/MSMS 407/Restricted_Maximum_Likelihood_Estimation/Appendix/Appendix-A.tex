\section{$\boldsymbol{Y}$ to OLS residuals}
\label{app:Y_to_residuals}

In OLS, 
$$\hat{\bs{\beta}} = (X'X)^{-1}X'\bs{Y},$$
\vspace{0.2cm}
$$\widehat{\bs{Y}} = X(X'X)^{-1}X'\bs{Y}.$$

Then,

\begin{align*}
\bs{e} &= \bs{Y} - \widehat{\bs{Y}} \\[0.5em]
&= \bs{Y} - X(X'X)^{-1}X'\bs{Y} \\[0.5em]
&= \left[ I - X(X'X)^{-1}X' \right] \bs{Y}.
\end{align*}
